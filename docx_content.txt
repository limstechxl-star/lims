[338] Frequently Asked Questions
[339] Find answers to common questions about LabAx pricing, features, and support.
[341] How does per-user pricing work?
[342] Our per-user pricing model charges based on the number of active users in your laboratory. Each user gets access to all features included in your selected plan. You can add or remove users at any time, and billing adjusts accordingly.
[344] Is my data secure?
[345] Yes, we take data security very seriously. LabAx uses industry-standard encryption, secure data centers, and regular security audits to protect your laboratory data. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
[346] Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
[347] Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated based on your current billing cycle. Our team is available to help you choose the right plan for your needs.
[348] What happens if I cancel my subscription?
[349] If you cancel your subscription, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will be downgraded to a read-only mode where you can export your data. We retain your data for 90 days before permanent deletion.
[350] What payment methods do you accept?
[351] We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoice-based billing with flexible payment terms.
[352] Do you offer free trials?
[353] Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period, and our support team is available to help you get started.
